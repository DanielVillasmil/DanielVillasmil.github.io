Let me share a little about myself!

I was born in Columbus, Ohio. Shortly after my family moved to Venezuela where I lived most of my life. While growing up I started playing different kinds of video games to which it slowly made me be more curious of how each component on a software level on computers and consoles worked. While studying Civil Engineering in Venezuela, I was met with C++ and that made it easy to find out that software development was my calling. After moving back to Columbus, I joined the USArmy where I served six years. Once I finished my contract I was in the lookout for a job and found We Can Code IT bootcamp where i became a software developer. My hobbies include playing video games, watching movies, and travelling.
